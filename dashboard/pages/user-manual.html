User Manual

  • Add a Farm

    Step 1:

    Login with your Email-Id. You can sign in with your Google Account and Facebook

    Step 2:

    Go to the Navigation Bar in the left pane and click on Add a Farm

    Step 3:

    Enter your details and submit a form to our analyst with the requirements.

  • Add a Device

    Step 1:

    Go to the Navigation Bar in the left pane and click on Add a Device.

    Step 2:

    Enter a short Description

    Step 3:

    Place a request! A analyst will get in touch with you shortly.

  • Add a Task

    Step 1:

    Once you are in a monitoring page, See the suggestions in the "Suggestions List".

    Step 2:

    Choose a suggestion you want to add to your To-do List. And Press "Add to List"

    Step 3:

    The task will be registered in the system and you can see it in the Global To Do List on your homescreen!
